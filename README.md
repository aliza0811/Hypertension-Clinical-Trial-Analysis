# Analysis of Hypertension Clinical Trials in India
This project analyses hypertension related clinical trial data conducted in India to identify trends in trial actiivity, study phases, sponsor participation, geographic distribution, and trial outcomes. 
The objective is to generate actionable insights that can support clinical research planning, sponsor strategy, and healthcare decision making.
## Objectives
To analyse trends, patterns, and operational characterization clinical trials in India, with emphasis on:
1. Trial volume over time
2. Distribution across clinical phases
3. Geographic concentration of studies
4. Sponsor activity
5. Study status and completion trends
## Business objectives
This project addresses the following analytical questions:
1. How has the number of hypertension clinical trials changed overtime?
2. Which clinical trial phases are most common?
3. Which cities and states conduct the highest number of trials?
4. Who are the leading sponsots of hypertension research?
5. What is the distribution of study types and recruitment status?
6. What is the average duration of clinical trials?
## Dataset source
Clinical trial records are collected from publicly available registry data - ClinicalTrials.gov.in
